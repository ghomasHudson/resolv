Getting Started

Follow the instructions below for your browser:

Google Chrome

  1. Right-click the address bar and click 'Edit search engines...'.
  2. Click the ADD button (Opposite the 'Other search engines' title)
  3. Fill in the form:

    Search engine
    resolv
    Keyword
    e.g. 'go' or 'res'
    URL
  4. Get resolving! (E.g. go bbc)

Firefox

  1. Right click the above search box and click "Add Keyword for this Search".
  2. Add a suitable keyword, eg. 'go' or 'res'
  3. Get resolving!
