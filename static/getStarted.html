Getting Started

Follow the instructions below for your browser:

Google Chrome

  1. Right-click the address bar and click 'Edit search engines'. This will display a list of your current engines
  2. Click the ADD button (Opposite the 'Other search engines' title)
  3. Fill in the form:
    Search engine: resolver
    Keyword: You can pick any keyword you like but most people use 'go' or 'res'.
    URL: [BASE_URL]/resolve?q=%s
